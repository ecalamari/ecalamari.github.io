About Me

As a graduate student at The Graduate Center, CUNY in New York City, I work with Dr. Jacqueline Faherty characterizing the atmospheres of brown dwarfs. Previously, I earned a Bachelor's degree in physics from Barnard College in NYC.

You can find a list of my publications via ADS.

Outside of my work, I fill my time surrounded by the arts -- visiting museums, hosting craft night and knitting sweaters for my friends and family.
